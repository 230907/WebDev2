ABOUT ASHLYN HARRIS

Currently she plays for the Orlando Pride NWSL team but has played for the NWSL since 2009. She was named NWSL Goalkeeper of the Year in 2016, she made 62 saves and had a 1.33 goals-against-average in 15 games. She had 4 clean sheets, and recorded 70 saves in the 2018 season. She also got NWSL Save of the week in the first week of the 2019 season.
